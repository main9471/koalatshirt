the Code of Ethics

Introduction

Koala T-shirt is committed to conducting its business with integrity, honesty, and fairness. This Code of Ethics outlines the values and behaviours expected of all staff, ensuring respect for customers, colleagues, and the community.

User Information

  • We respect the privacy of our customers and ensure that all personal information (e.g., name, address, email) is stored securely.
  • Information is only used for purposes such as order processing, customer communication, and promotional updates.
  • Our approach follows the guidelines outlined in our Privacy Policy.

Access and Equity

  • Koala T-shirt provides equal service and employment opportunities regardless of race, gender, age, religion, or disability.
  • We aim to make our physical store and website accessible and inclusive for everyone.

Fair Practices

  • We only sell legally sourced products from trusted suppliers.
  • All marketing and advertising will be truthful and misleading.
  • We ensure honest communication with customers and treat all feedback with respect.

Grievance Procedure

Koala T-shirt values feedback from both staff and customers. If you have a concern or complaint, please follow these simple steps:

  • Talk it through
    If possible, try to resolve the issue directly with the person involved.
  • Submit a complaint
    If the issue remains, staff can speak to a supervisor, and customers can contact us via email or our contact form. Please include clear details.
