Koala T-shirt

Founded in 1992 by Koah La and Tee Shar, Koala T-shirt began as a small shop in Sydney CBD. We sell quality, affordable t-shirts for kids, men, women, and plus sizes. All items are carefully selected and sourced from T-shirt4All in Cabramatta. Our goal is to bring comfort, style, and friendly service to every customer — now available online for your convenience.
