Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 08, 2025

Koala T-shirt is committed to protecting your privacy.
This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information on our website, in line with principles of confidentiality, integrity, availability, and security.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name (first and last)
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Mailing address (including city, state, ZIP/postal code)
  • Payment and billing information
  • Order history and preferences
  • Device and browser information
  • Usage data including visit time, pages viewed, and interaction data
  • Cookies and tracking data

2. How We Use the Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To process orders and payments securely
  • To create and manage your user account
  • To provide customer support
  • To send order confirmations, delivery updates, and other transactional emails
  • To send promotional offers and marketing communications (with your consent)
  • To improve our website and shopping experience
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect against fraud or misuse

3. Disclosure of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share it with trusted third parties under the following conditions:

  • With service providers (e.g., payment processors, delivery partners) who assist in operating our business
  • With legal or regulatory authorities if required by law
  • In connection with a business transfer such as a merger or acquisition

All third parties are required to maintain the confidentiality and security of your data.

4. Controlling Your Information and Web Security

We implement security measures to ensure the integrity and confidentiality of your personal data:

  • SSL encryption for secure data transmission
  • Password-protected user accounts
  • Regular software updates and security patches
  • Limited access to sensitive data, granted only to authorized personnel

You can access, correct, or delete your information by contacting us at support@koalatshirt.com.

5. Integrity, Confidentiality, Availability & Security of Data

  • Integrity: We ensure that your data is accurate, up-to-date, and protected from unauthorized alterations.
  • Confidentiality: We use encryption and access controls to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Availability: Our servers and systems are maintained to ensure service availability and data recovery in case of failure.
  • Security: We regularly assess risks and update our practices to prevent breaches and data loss.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us at:
